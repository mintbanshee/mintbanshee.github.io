🪐 StellarNest


This branding concept was created for *StellarNest*, a futuristic space-tech incubator that began as a think tank for off-Earth innovation. The client requested a minimalist logo featuring
a nest of orbit lines surrounding a central star or planet, with a palette of deep blue, silver, and orange. I designed this abstract solar system to represent innovation, launch momentum, and collaborative gravity.

Using Illustrator’s width tool, I shaped the orbital paths to create a subtle sense of depth and intentional flow—hinting at layered ideas and shared direction.
The result is a clean, balanced composition that aligns with the brief while staying true to my style: symbolic, story-driven, and orbiting around meaning.


Illustrator Visual Metaphor Orbit Path Typography Width Tool Perspective
