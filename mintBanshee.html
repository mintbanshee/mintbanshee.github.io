MintBanshee

Personal Brand Identity • Illustrator • Concept + Logo Design

A custom brand mark created to represent my MintBanshee identity. The logo blends a crescent moon with botanical elements and { } brackets, merging themes of creativity, growth, and code. Designed as a clean vector in Illustrator, the symbol explores shape balance, hand-drawn curves, and subtle ornamentation to form a soft, modern monogram.

Below is a work in progress image and two (of the many) early black-and-white variations used during the design process while refining structure, flow, and overall silhouette.

~ ✧ ~

The completed MintBanshee branding package includes:

• Primary logo (full colour) – crescent, sprout, { } curly braces, and sparkles
• Monochrome logo – optimized for print, engraving, stickers, and minimal contexts
• SVG master file – resolution-independent for web, app icons, and animation
• Favicon icon (16px/32px/48px) – the tiny moon-sprout mark used in browser tabs
• Profile-scale version – simplified for social media, GitHub, and avatars
• Background-safe version – with circular field for use on gradients or photography

Each piece was designed to remain clean, readable, and recognizable at any size,
from full-width hero images all the way down to the miniature 16-pixel favicon.
