StellarNest

Branding Exploration • Illustrator • Concept Development

A logo design assignment built around an imagined space-tech incubator. The goal was to create a minimalist mark using orbit paths to form a subtle “nest” around a central star, symbolizing innovation, collaboration, and launch-ready ideas.

The project went through multiple iterations—beginning with a more abstract, bird-nest-inspired approach, then refined through instructor feedback to reduce visual clutter, unify orbit planes, and improve slogan placement. The final version balances clarity with motion, using deep blues and orange to reflect technology, creativity, and ignition.
